## Introduction

Thank you for participating in this user evaluation of NextTrack, a privacy-focused music recommendation system. Your feedback will help assess the quality and usability of this prototype.

**What you'll be doing:**
1. Using the NextTrack web interface to get music recommendations
2. Evaluating the quality of the recommendations
3. Answering questions about your experience

**Privacy Note:** This study does not collect any personally identifiable information. Your responses will be anonymized and used only for academic evaluation purposes.
